# Meta-analysis_of_alfalfa_response_to_water_management_for_yield_and_quality
Code and data for a meta-analysis quantifying irrigation effects on alfalfa yield, forage quality, and water productivity across the United States.
This repository contains the data and code used for a meta-analysis evaluating the effects of irrigation water management on alfalfa (Medicago sativa L.) yield,
crude protein (CP), irrigation water use efficiency (IWUE), and total water productivity (TWP) across the United States.
The meta-analysis identified irrigation thresholds, quantified trade-offs between yield and forage quality, and evaluated how climate, soil, and management factors influence alfalfa responses to water limitation.

📌 Overview

This repository contains the data and code used for a meta-analysis evaluating the effects of irrigation water management on alfalfa (Medicago sativa L.) yield, crude protein (CP), irrigation water use efficiency (IWUE), and total water productivity (TWP) across the United States.

The study synthesizes 51 years of data (1971–2022) to identify irrigation thresholds, quantify trade-offs between yield and forage quality, and evaluate how climate, soil, and management factors influence alfalfa responses to water limitation.
